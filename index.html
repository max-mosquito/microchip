Analog Circuit Layout

Experienced in all levels of complexity from cell level to top level.

Extensive experience with analog circuit layout for high voltage, mixed-signal, automotive ASICs on bipolar, CMOS and BCD technologies.

High voltage, RF requirements, matching strategies, shielding, sensitive route considerations, etc.

Using tools for the design from Cadence  and Mentor Graphics.

Digital Physical Design

Using tools for the design by IC Compiler  from Synopsys.

Place and route, CTS, RC extraction, STA, timing closure and physical design verification.
